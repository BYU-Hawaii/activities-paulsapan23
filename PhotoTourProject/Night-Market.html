Quadro Foodie Market was founded by 3 enthusiastic people who shared the same goal of opening a weekend food market in the province of Leyte. In its firrst eveerr season, Quadro opened its door to the public in the city of beautiful people, Ormoc and catered to young professionals and other workers of the city.

The founders said that the idea was inspired by similarly-styled food markets in Cebu and Manila.

The Quadro Night Market Experience: A Blend of Shopping and Culture Step into the vibrant tapestry of Ormoc’s cultural heritage at the Quadro Night Market, where every moment is infused with the warmth and hospitality of its people. Engage with locals, immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere, and discover the stories behind each stall and item. Whether you’re seeking unique souvenirs, delectable street food, or simply a taste of local culture, the Quadro Night Market offers an experience like no other.
